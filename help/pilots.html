General Description:

The pilot roster window is for creating and managing your pilots. Keep in mind if you setup a new pilot first adjust the pilot type and gender, cause both selections are locked after you saved and loaded your pilot again.


Printing:

If you hit the print icon while the pilot window is open, your pilot is printed in separate page. Not only his stats, like in the aircraft printout, but also his feats, appearance, etc.


Pilot Type:

You may chooes between Hero, Sidekick, Co-Pilot/Gunner and NPC. Your choice affects the starting EPs and the validation rules for this pilot.

A Hero or Sidekick are the two possible pilots you know from the official rules.
