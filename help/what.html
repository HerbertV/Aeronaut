What is Aeronaut?


Back to Index


AERONAUT is an unoffical custom aircraft design and editing tool for the out-of-print CRIMSON SKIES boardgame by created FASA.
CRIMSON SKIES is © 2009 by Smith & Tinker
(previous rights owner: Microsoft Corporation and FASA). Inspired by Scott Janssens' CADET.


AERONAUT features all official Crimson Skies Boardgame rules, including Autogyros and Special Characteristics, which where missing in CADET. It supports the FF5 Houserules. You can easy switch between official rules and custom rules


Also AERONAUT is expandable, which means you can add new weapons, rockets, etc. by your self.


AERONAUT was programmed by Herbert "Mad MaxX" of Fox Force Five. So if you want to conact me visit:
www.foxforcefive.de/cs.
